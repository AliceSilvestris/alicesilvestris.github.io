Scottish Borders

Scottish Borders

A day to remember with Monks, Lords and Poets

Your day starts with a visit to the remarkable 15th century Rosslyn Chapel. From here you head south into the Borders and the stunning views at Scott’s View. Dryburgh Abbey is the next stop before visiting the border town of Melrose and a chance to stop for lunch. For the afternoon you have the choice of Abbotsford House, Floors Castle or Traquair House.

Price(total for 1 or 7 guests)

1-7 guests £450(does not include entry to attractions)

Tour duration approximately 8 -9 hours.
