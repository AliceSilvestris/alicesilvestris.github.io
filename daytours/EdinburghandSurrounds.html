Edinburgh and More

The Heart of Lothian. Royalty, Palaces and Fantasy

A morning drive around Edinburgh’s New and Old Town plus the Royal Holyrood Park. Then on to the 15th century Rosslyn Chapel to crack the Da Vinci Code. Lunch in the ancient village of South Queensferry with views of the Forth Bridge. The Afternoon starts with a visit to Linlithgow Palace, birth place of Mary Queen of Scots. A short Journey to Blackness Castle and if time allows a visit to the stately Hopetoun House.

Price (does not include entry to attractions)

1-7 guests £450

Tour duration approximately 8 hours.
