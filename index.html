About Us

We want you to enjoy a magical experience in Scotland, taking wonderful memories home with you.

MS Tours is a family run business. We are based in the beautiful city of Edinburgh. Let us take you on an unforgettable journey through the highways and byways of Scotland. All our tours are customised to your needs. This is your trip so we move at your pace. We can provide one day or multi-day tours to all corners of Scotland. we want to make your visit to Scotland truly special.
