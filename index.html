Edinburgh and Surrounds

We start with a drive around Arthur’s Seat before heading to the 15th century Rosslyn Chapel, made famous by the Da Vinci code. Then we head to the majestic Linlithgow Palace and Blackness Castle before exploring the ancient South Queensferry and its famous bridges. In the afternoon we can explore Scotland’s largest stately home at Hopetoun House.

Tour duration approximately 8 hours.

St Andrews

It’s not just the golf on offer here as the ancient Abbey, Castle and the University buildings make it a memorable day. You can also take a walk over the Swilcan Bridge. On heading back to Edinburgh why not make a stop at the stunning Falkland Palace, a onetime home to the Stewart Kings and Queens. If you wish, we can also take you to the Kingsbarns Distillery or offer the Eden Mill Gin tour.

Tour duration approximately 8 -9 hours
