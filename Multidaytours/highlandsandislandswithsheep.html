Four, Five, Six days or more…..Your choice.

Multi-Day tours are tailored to suit what you want to experience. Below is a suggested 4-day tour taking in the Highlands and the island of Skye. If you wish to spend more time with us, we can explore more of our islands such as Mull, Iona, Orkney or even the Outer Hebrides

Timeline of tour

    Leaving from Edinburgh we travel north west, making stops at South Queensferry, Stirling Castle, Doune Castle, Glen Etive and Glencoe before resting for the night in Fort William.

    After breakfast we head for Neptune’s Staircase and Glenfinnan Viaduct taking the road to the Isles. We take in the Silver Sands of Morar before reaching Mallaig and sailing to Skye. After exploring the south of Skye, we head for Portree to end the day.

    A circuit of the north of Skye with stops at the Old Man of Storr, Kilt Rock, Quiraing, Black Cottages, Fairy Glen, Dunvegan Castle, Talisker Distillery and back to Portree.

    Leaving Skye we visit Eilean Donan Castle, drive over Ratagan Pass to Loch Ness before heading to Dalwhinnie Whisky Distillery then back south to Edinburgh.

If wishing to extend this tour beyond 4 days then this allows us to get to even more of Scotland’s stunning scenery. Areas such as Inverness to visit Culloden Battlefield and the Clava Cairns or why not explore more of our islands such as Mull, Iona, Orkney or even the Outer Hebrides.

Please note the above is just a guide and the day can be tailored to suit.
