Timeline of tour

This tour can be tailored to suit but some of the highlights we can visit are:

Leave Edinburgh and our first stop is the village of Queensferry. Next we head to the mighty Stirling Castle and if you are interested in Monty Python or Outlander then Doune Castle is our next stop. We then head north to explore Glencoe and the Glen Etive area before heading to the Glenfinnan Viaduct made famous in the Harry Potter movies. Fort William for the evening.

We head to Spean Bridge for views of Ben Nevis before returning south through the Cairngorms National Park. We can visit Blair Castle and even take in a Highlands whisky tour. A stop at Pitlochry before returning to Edinburgh.

Please note the above is just a guide and the day can be tailored to suit.
