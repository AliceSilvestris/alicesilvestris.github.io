Castles, Whisky and Magnificent Scenery

Below are some suggestions for your 2 days. The tour can be tailored to suit you.

Timeline of tour:

Leaving Edinburgh we head to our first stop, the village of Queensferry. A pitstop at the Kelpies, on our way to the magnificent Stirling Castle. Doune Castle is next for those with an interest in Monty Python or Outlander. Perhaps a stop for lunch as we head northwards to Glencoe and Glen Etive, before reaching Fort William for the evening.

After breakfast we head to the Glenfinnan Viaduct made famous in the Harry Potter movies. Next on your journey we travel to Spean Bridge for spectacular views of Ben Nevis before a whisky tour at Dalwhinnie Distillery. We now head south through the Cairngorms to take in Blair Castle before stopping at Dunkeld then returning to Edinburgh
